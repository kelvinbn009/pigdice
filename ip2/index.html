The Best Place In Town

Situated 4 kilometres from the city centre, The Carnivore opened its doors in September 1980 to instant success.
The food, service and atmosphere are strikingly different from anything ever seen in Kenya.
The Carnivore is a meat specialty restaurant and it is referred to as 'the ultimate 'Beast of a Feast'.
